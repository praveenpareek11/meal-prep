## Recipes

**Weekend prep components** (make once, use all week): masala base, boiled legumes, boiled dal + tadka, boiled potatoes, veggie boxes, curd, sprouts, rotis/theplas, sambar-style dal.

**Weekday dishes** (25-45 min, morning): rajma/chole from components, soya bhurji, matar paneer, lauki kootu, pav bhaji, kadai vegetable, dalia pulao, pongal.

Browse the full list in the **Recipes** section of the nav. Every recipe states its fridge life, freezer-safety, and active time up top.
