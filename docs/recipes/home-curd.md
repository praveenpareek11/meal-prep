# Home Curd

**Type:** weekend-prep  
**Fridge life:** 4-5 days · **Freezer-safe:** no (never freeze)  
**Active time:** 10 min active + overnight set · **Makes:** 1 L batch, twice a week  
**Uses prepped components:** none  
**Source:** own, validated  
**Kitchen-tested:** ❌ not yet  

1. Boil 1 L milk, then simmer 2-3 min for thicker curd.
2. Cool to warm (finger comfortably tolerable ≈ 40-45°C).
3. In a small bowl, whisk 1-2 tsp fresh starter curd with 2 tbsp warm milk till smooth.
4. Add this starter mix back to the milk and stir once.
5. Cover and leave undisturbed for 6-8 hrs or overnight in a warm but not hot place. In humid/warm weather, check earlier if possible.
6. Once set, fridge immediately. Use within 4-5 days. Set a fresh batch twice a week (e.g., prep day + Wednesday night — 5 min of work).
