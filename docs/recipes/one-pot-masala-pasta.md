# One-Pot Masala Fusilli Pasta — 1 person, 1 meal

**Type:** weekday-dish  
**Fridge life:** 1 day best in monsoon · **Freezer-safe:** No  
**Active time:** 20-25 min (morning) · **Makes:** 1 meal portion  
**Uses prepped components:** none — fresh one-pot dish  
**Source:** own  

Best method is the **steel pan with glass lid**. The pressure cooker works too, but pasta can become softer.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient                              |                                Quantity |
| --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------: |
| Fusilli pasta                            | **85 g** (≈1-1.1 cups loose); weigh — cup volume varies by shape/brand |
| Oil or butter                            |                                  1 tbsp |
| Garlic                                   |             3-4 cloves, finely chopped |
| Onion                                    |                        1 small, chopped |
| Tomato                                   |             1 medium, chopped or grated |
| Green chilli                             |                        1, slit/chopped |
| Capsicum / frozen peas-carrot-beans      |                        ¼ cup, optional |
| Salt                                     |                    ¾ tsp, adjust later |
| Haldi                                    |                       1 pinch, optional |
| Red chilli powder                        |                                   ¼ tsp |
| Black pepper                             |                                   ¼ tsp |
| Mixed herbs / oregano                    |                        ½ tsp, optional |
| Tomato ketchup                           |                         1 tsp, optional |
| Water                                    | **1¼ cups** (pan) / **1 cup** (cooker) |
| Cheese                                   |    1-2 cubes grated or 2 tbsp grated |

## Method 1: Steel pan with glass lid — recommended

1. Heat **1 tbsp oil/butter** in the steel pan.
2. Add chopped garlic + green chilli. Sauté for **30 seconds**.
3. Add chopped onion. Cook for **2-3 minutes** till slightly soft.
4. Add chopped/grated tomato.
5. Add salt, red chilli powder, black pepper, optional haldi, and optional mixed herbs.
6. Cook for **3-4 minutes** till tomato becomes soft and saucy.
7. Add optional frozen peas-carrot-beans/capsicum. Cook for **1 minute**.
8. Add fusilli pasta.
9. Add **1¼ cups water** and mix well.
10. Cover with glass lid.
11. Cook on **medium-low flame for 10-12 minutes**, stirring every **3-4 minutes** so it does not stick.
12. Once pasta is cooked and sauce is thick, switch off flame.
13. Rest covered for **2 minutes**.
14. Add grated cheese while pasta is hot.
15. Mix lightly and serve.

## Method 2: Pressure cooker one-pot pasta

1. Heat **1 tbsp oil/butter** in the pressure cooker.
2. Add chopped garlic + green chilli. Sauté for **30 seconds**.
3. Add chopped onion. Cook for **2-3 minutes** till slightly soft.
4. Add chopped/grated tomato.
5. Add salt, red chilli powder, black pepper, optional haldi, and optional mixed herbs.
6. Cook for **3-4 minutes** till tomato becomes soft and saucy.
7. Add optional frozen peas-carrot-beans/capsicum. Cook for **1 minute**.
8. Add fusilli pasta.
9. Add **1 cup water** and mix well. Pasta should be just about covered, not drowned.
10. Close the pressure cooker lid.
11. Cook on **medium flame for 1 whistle only**.
12. Switch off flame immediately after 1 whistle.
13. Wait **3-4 minutes**, then release pressure carefully.
14. Open and mix gently.
15. If it looks watery, simmer open for **1-2 minutes**.
16. Switch off flame.
17. Add grated cheese while pasta is hot.
18. Mix lightly and serve.

## Cheese timing

Add cheese **after cooking, while serving hot**.

Do **not** add cheese during pressure cooking or while boiling because it can stick, split, or make the pasta heavy. For pan method also, adding at the end gives better texture.

## If making lunch + dinner same day

Double everything:

| Ingredient    |                                    Quantity |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------: |
| Fusilli pasta |                **170 g** (≈2-2.2 cups loose) |
| Water         | **2¼-2½ cups** (pan) / **2 cups** (cooker) |

Cool within **30-40 minutes**, refrigerate, and reheat thoroughly before dinner.
