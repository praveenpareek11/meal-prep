# Plain Moong Dal — Dhuli + Chhilka Mix

**Type:** weekday-dish  
**Fridge life:** same day best; dinner portion safe if cooled and refrigerated within 30-40 min · **Freezer-safe:** Yes, but not needed for same day  
**Active time:** 15-20 min (morning) · **Makes:** 2 bowls dal = lunch + dinner for 1 person  
**Uses prepped components:** none  
**Source:** own  
**Kitchen-tested:** ❌ not yet  

*Mix ratio: 70% dhuli moong + 30% moong chilka. Pairs with 6 rotis + aloo pyaaz tamatar sabzi. Make it slightly thin because moong dal thickens by dinner.*

## Ingredients

| Ingredient       | Quantity                             |
| ---------------- | -----------------------------------: |
| Dhuli moong dal  | ⅓ cup                                |
| Moong chilka dal | 2 tbsp                               |
| Water            | 2.25 to 2.5 cups                     |
| Salt             | ¾ tsp, adjust                        |
| Haldi            | ¼ tsp                                |
| Ghee or oil      | 1 tsp                                |
| Jeera            | ¾ tsp                                |
| Hing             | 1 pinch optional                     |
| Green chilli     | 1 slit optional                      |
| Ginger           | ½ tsp grated optional                |
| Lemon            | few drops optional, add while eating |

## Process

1. Wash both dals 3-4 times until water is mostly clear.
2. Soak for **10 minutes** if you have time. Optional, but helps it cook evenly.
3. Add dal to pressure cooker with:

    - **2.25 to 2.5 cups water**
    - **¼ tsp haldi**
    - **¾ tsp salt**

4. Pressure cook:

    - **2 whistles on medium flame**
    - Then switch off and let pressure release naturally for 5-10 min.

5. Open and whisk lightly with a spoon. Keep it slightly loose. Add hot water if too thick.
6. For tadka, heat **1 tsp ghee/oil** in small tadka pan.
7. Add **jeera + hing + green chilli + ginger**.
8. Let jeera crackle, then pour tadka into dal.
9. Simmer dal for **2-3 min** after tadka.
10. Taste salt. Add lemon only in your bowl while eating, not in the whole dal if storing for dinner.

## Storage for dinner

- Take out dinner portion before eating lunch.
- Cool **30-40 min max**, then refrigerate covered.
- Reheat in a small pan with **2-4 tbsp water** because it will thicken by evening.
- Bring it to a proper boil before eating.
