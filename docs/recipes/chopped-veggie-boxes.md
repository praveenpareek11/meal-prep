## 5. Chopped Veggie Boxes

**Type:** weekend-prep
**Fridge life:** 3-4 days (plan to consume by day 3) · **Freezer-safe:** no
**Active time:** 20-30 min chopping · **Makes:** 1 week of hardy veg
**Uses prepped components:** none
**Source:** own, validated

**On prep day:** wash veggies before chopping, drain well, then **dry completely** on a cloth (moisture = monsoon spoilage). Chop only after the surface is dry. Store in airtight boxes with a paper towel/tissue at the bottom to absorb condensation. Keep boxes on the cold fridge shelf, not in the door.

| Veggie | Chop ahead? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Lauki, toru, kaddu | ✅ 3-4 days | Peel, chop, dry again if watery, then box with tissue |
| Gobi | ✅ 3-4 days | Cut florets, rinse/check well, dry very thoroughly before boxing |
| Gawar fali, beans | ✅ 3-4 days | String + chop; keep very dry |
| Onion | ✅ 2-3 days | Peel and chop dry; do not wash after chopping; separate sealed box (smell) |
| Tomato | ❌ | Goes watery — chop fresh (2 min) |
| **Bhindi** | ❌ **The exception** | Wash a day ahead, dry FULLY on cloth, store whole, chop fresh (5 min on dry bhindi) |
| Brinjal | ❌ | Browns when cut; cook fresh only |

Frozen peas/carrot/beans need nothing — they're already prepped. Your emergency arsenal.
