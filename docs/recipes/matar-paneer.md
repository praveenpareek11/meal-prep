# Matar Paneer

**Type:** weekday-dish  
**Fridge life:** 1 day · **Freezer-safe:** no  
**Morning active time:** 20 min · **Serves:** 1 person, lunch + dinner  
**Uses prepped components:** onion-tomato masala base — 150 g / 4 heaped tbsp  
**Source:** Hebbars Kitchen Matar Paneer, adapted for prepped masala base  

## Ingredients

| Ingredient               | Quantity                                        |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------: |
| Paneer                   | 200 g, cubes (150 g is sufficient on non-gym weeks) |
| Frozen peas              | ¾ cup                                           |
| Onion-tomato masala base | 150 g / 4 heaped tbsp                           |
| Oil or ghee              | 2 tsp                                           |
| Jeera                    | ½ tsp                                           |
| Ginger-garlic paste      | ½ tsp, optional                                 |
| Haldi                    | ¼ tsp                                           |
| Red chilli powder        | ½ tsp                                           |
| Dhania powder            | ½ tsp                                           |
| Garam masala             | ½ tsp                                           |
| Kasuri methi             | ½ tsp, crushed                                  |
| Salt                     | ½ tsp or to taste                               |
| Water                    | ¾ cup                                           |
| Cream                    | 1 tbsp, optional                                |
| Coriander                | 1 tbsp, optional                                |

## Method
1. If paneer is store-bought and firm, soak cubes in warm water for 5 min, then drain.
2. Heat oil or ghee in kadai. Add jeera and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté 30 sec.
3. Add onion-tomato masala base, haldi, red chilli powder, dhania powder, and salt. Cook 3-4 min.
4. Add frozen peas and ¾ cup water. Cover and simmer 6-7 min.
5. Add paneer cubes and kasuri methi. Simmer gently for 3-4 min only.
6. Add garam masala and optional cream. Mix once and switch off.

## Portion & Store
- Split into 2 portions: lunch + dinner.
- Cool 30-40 min, then refrigerate the dinner portion.
- Reheat gently in kadai with 1-2 tbsp water; avoid hard boiling after paneer is added.
- Eat same day; do not freeze.
- Buy fresh paneer weekly and use opened paneer within 2-3 days; do not keep opened paneer loosely in the fridge.

## What it becomes / variations
- Add carrot-beans mix, but keep paneer and peas as the main bulk.
