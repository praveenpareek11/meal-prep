## 4. Boiled Potatoes

**Type:** weekend-prep
**Fridge life:** 3-4 days · **Freezer-safe:** no (never freeze)
**Active time:** 1 cooker cycle, zero attention · **Makes:** 5-6 medium potatoes
**Uses prepped components:** none
**Source:** own, validated

**Batch:** 5-6 medium potatoes | **Time:** 1 pressure-cooker cycle (3 whistles), zero attention

1. Wash potatoes well. Keep skin ON.
2. Add 1 cup water to the pressure cooker. Place potatoes directly in the cooker or on a separator/stand if available.
3. Pressure cook for 3 whistles on medium flame. For very large potatoes, use 4 whistles.
4. Let pressure release naturally.
5. Check doneness with a knife: it should slide in easily, but potatoes should not be collapsing.
6. Remove potatoes from the cooker water and cool fully on a plate/colander so surface moisture dries.
7. Store **unpeeled** in a steel dabba. Fridge 3-4 days. **Never freeze** (texture turns grainy).

**Becomes:** jeera aloo (5 min), aloo-mangodi, aloo added to poha, quick bhaji mash with masala base, aloo raita with your curd.
