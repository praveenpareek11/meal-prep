# Aloo Pyaaz Tamatar Sabzi

**Type:** weekday-dish  
**Fridge life:** same day best; dinner portion safe if cooled and refrigerated within 30-40 min · **Freezer-safe:** No, because potato texture spoils  
**Active time:** 20-25 min (morning) · **Makes:** 2 meals for 1 person  
**Uses prepped components:** none  
**Source:** own  
**Kitchen-tested:** ❌ not yet  

*Style: semi-dry sabzi, not watery gravy. Pairs with 6 rotis + plain moong dal.*

## Ingredients

| Ingredient        | Quantity                                 |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------: |
| Potato            | 2 medium, ~280-300 g                     |
| Onion             | 1 medium, finely chopped or thin sliced  |
| Tomato            | 1 medium, finely chopped                 |
| Green chilli      | 1, slit/chopped optional                 |
| Oil               | 1.5 tbsp                                 |
| Jeera             | ¾ tsp                                    |
| Hing              | 1 pinch optional                         |
| Haldi             | ¼ tsp                                    |
| Red chilli powder | ½ tsp                                    |
| Dhania powder     | 1 tsp                                    |
| Salt              | ¾ tsp, adjust                            |
| Garam masala      | ¼ tsp                                    |
| Amchur            | ¼ tsp optional                           |
| Water             | 3-5 tbsp                                 |

## Process

1. Peel potatoes and cut into small cubes, around **1-1.5 cm**. Smaller cubes cook faster.
2. Heat **1.5 tbsp oil** in kadai.
3. Add **jeera + hing**. Let jeera crackle.
4. Add **onion + green chilli + a pinch of salt**. Cook on medium flame for **4-5 min** until onion turns light golden.
5. Add **tomato + haldi + red chilli + dhania powder + salt**.
6. Cook **4-5 min** until tomato becomes soft and oil starts separating slightly.
7. Add potato cubes. Mix well for **1-2 min** so masala coats the potatoes.
8. Add **3-5 tbsp water**, cover, and cook on low-medium flame for **10-12 min**.
9. Open and check. If potatoes are not soft, sprinkle 1-2 tbsp more water and cook covered for another **3-5 min**.
10. Once potatoes are cooked, cook uncovered for **2-3 min** to make it semi-dry.
11. Add **garam masala + amchur**. Mix and switch off.

## Storage for dinner

- Remove dinner portion before eating lunch.
- Cool **30-40 min max**, then refrigerate in a covered box.
- Reheat in kadai with **1-2 tbsp water**, covered for 3-4 min, then dry it slightly.
