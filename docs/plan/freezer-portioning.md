# Freezer Portioning Guide

- Freeze in **single-meal portions** — you'll never defrost more than you need.
- Cool cooked food first, but don't leave it sitting out for hours. Move to fridge/freezer once steam has stopped and food is warm, not hot.
- Use flat/shallow containers; they chill, freeze, and thaw faster than deep boxes.
- Leave a little headspace for dals, legumes, and sambar-dal because liquids expand slightly when frozen.
- Label with masking tape + marker: item + date. (Sounds fussy; saves you from mystery boxes by Thursday.)
- Thawing without a microwave: move tomorrow's portion from freezer → fridge the night before, or drop the frozen block straight into the kadai on low with a splash of water and reheat till piping hot. 🔲 With a microwave: defrost 2-3 min, then reheat till fully hot.
- Freezer-safe: legumes, dals, masala base, sambar-dal, theplas. **Not freezer-safe:** boiled potato, curd, cooked rice, cut raw veggies (except the ones you bought pre-frozen).
